OSS Sustainability, not subsistence

  • Shelfware won't help anyone.
  • Several competing models for OSS sustainability:
    • Professional services model
    • Software-as-a-service model
    • Open core model
  • The WRF Fellowship is uniquely suited to help me navigate this landscape and foster the sustainability of our software suite.
- We want sustainability, not subsistence - Professional services (sell training, consulting, etc.) - SaaS (subscriptions for accounts, server access, cloud resources) - Open core (dual licensing model)
