A vision for tractometrics

(admittedly techo-utopian)
  • Imagine a world with cheap and ubiquitous MRI.
  • Individualized assessment of white matter will enable
    • tract-specific localization of white matter lesions,
    • localization of tracts for surgical planning,
    • ongoing assessment of white matter maturation,
    • tractometry-informed intervention.
  • We need tools that will spur innovation and transition to industry.
  • We need to clarify the white matter underpinning of important conditions and experiences.
