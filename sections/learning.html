Results

Classifying ALS

Using data from Sarica et al. (2017), Accuracy: 93±2%, ROC-AUC: 0.978±0.006

Predicting Age

Using 77 subjects aged 6-50 from Yeatman et al. (2014)

Median absolute error: 3.6 years, R2~0.3
